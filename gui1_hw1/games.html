Games

I've played alot of games in my life. I consider it essential part of my profile and personality.
I wouldn't be the way I am without them. Here are my top picks.

Team Fortress 2

A free to play multiplayer game that came out in 2007, and still has over 80,000 concurrent players on an average weekday. Wide character class, balanced gameplay, arena style and objective style playlists. Offline bots to practice, and custom map-making and matching making to top the cake. There is a reason why TF2 has been such a successful game.

Halo

One of the first story driven fps shooters in the market, first launching in 2001. A story about a super human soldier tasked with saving humanity from an alien armada spanding multiple galaxies and centuries. Campaign and multiplayer packed into one game consisteny bringing back players with story and gameplay.

Maplestory

A MMORPG, massive multiplayer online role-playing game, in 2D form with platforming, party quests, bossing, minigames, events, and tons of lore and story content. Over 40 classes to try out and play. Play progression solo or play with friends. Runs on any computer with decent hardware from 2010.

Genshin Impact

A large expansive open-world adventure game with many characters and places to explore, level up, and become stronger. Supports singleplayer and coop activities with fishing, housing, events, puzzles, world quests, and world bosses to defeat and enjoy gameplay.

Chess

Good old 200 year old game of 1vs1 content filled with many varieties to play and strategize. Put your opponent in checkmate by forcing your enemy's king into check without any way to defend. No two games are the same.
